### PB-X — micro-PB (new bucket, spawned from this reclassification)

Three (or four) primitives, ~100-150 LOC total:

1. `EffectFilter::AllCreaturesExcludingSubtype` (and chosen-subtype variant) — wires
   exclusion pump that already exists for "your creatures" to all-player scope.
   Unblocks Crippling Fear, Eyeblight Massacre, Olivia's Wrath.
2. `LayerModification::ModifyBoth { power: PTAmount, toughness: PTAmount }` where
   `PTAmount` accepts either a constant or an `EffectAmount` (e.g., count of Vampires
   you control). Unblocks Olivia's Wrath; also opens up future CDA-on-others pumps.
3. `Cost::ExileSelf` — generic activation-cost exile-self. Unblocks Balthor the
   Defiled and likely other legendary utility cards.
4. *(Optional)* Type-adding layer grant if Metallic Mimic's self-type wording isn't
   already expressible via `AdditionalTypes` on `EntersBattlefieldAs`.

Heritage Druid stays blocked — `TapNCreatures` is a much larger cost-framework change
and should live in its own PB, not PB-X.
